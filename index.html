About the App

Piggy-Piggy is designed to help parents and individuals manage money effectively. Imagine having two children, Kai and Freya, who both received $100 from grandpa. They are too young to track or handle a one-hundred dollar bill, so the parent keeps the money, but tracks their expenses in this simple to use app.

Family-Friendly Money Management

Perfect for parents managing allowances and gifts for their children.

Track Everything

Monitor money given, spent, and saved with detailed notes for each transaction.

On-The-Go Tracking

Manage money during trips, birthdays, or daily life with ease.
